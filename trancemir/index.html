TranceMir

is a fan-made, seasonal private server based on Mir 3 (Zircon), rebuilt with custom systems, events, and progression designed around fresh starts and evolving gameplay.

Each season runs for a limited time, after which the server goes offline and later returns with new content, system changes, balance adjustments, and progression paths — bringing a fresh experience every cycle.

TranceMir focuses on meaningful progression, event-driven content, and a community-first experience, while staying true to the core Mir 3 foundation that long-time players recognize and enjoy.

TranceMir is developed and maintained by Trancefied (TranceMirGM), for fans, and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any official Mir 3 or Legend of Mir publisher or rights holder.
