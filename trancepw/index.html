TrancePW

TrancePW is a fan-made, private server designed to recreate the classic Perfect World experience from the early 2010s, inspired by the gameplay, pacing, and systems many players remember from the early days.

The goal is to recreate that old-school feel while gradually refining and expanding it through quality-of-life improvements. Development is active and ongoing, and as a beta project, features and progress may change, reset, or evolve as the server continues to grow.

This project is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Arc Games, Perfect World Entertainment, or Perfect World International in any way. All original trademarks and assets remain the property of their respective owners.

If you’re looking to relive the classic Perfect World experience from around PW's golden age, explore familiar systems in a fresh environment, and take part in a server shaped through ongoing development and community feedback—welcome home.
